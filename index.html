    Our Story

    Nine decades of healing, one family's unbroken promise

    1932

    The Foundation

    Hakeem Shaikh Mohammad Hakeem Chichi established the pharmacy in Surat's historic Ranitalao area. His son Hakim M. Qasim continued and formalized the practice, creating a legacy that would span generations.

    1970s

    Growing the Legacy

    The pharmacy expanded its product range to include a comprehensive line of Sharbats, Murabbas, Gulkand, Chyawanprash, and specialized herbal preparations. Hakeem Chichi Street in Surat became synonymous with authentic herbal medicine.

    2000

    Recognition & Awards

    Awarded the prestigious Ujas Award 2000, followed by the Udhyog Patra Samman Puraskar 2009, India Gold Star Award 2010, and Gujarat Mitra Award – Surat na Sitara 2011.

    2010s

    Digital & National Expansion

    Hakeem Chichi launched its first website and mobile app, bringing authentic Unani and Ayurvedic products to customers across India and internationally. GMP certification was achieved, ensuring world-class manufacturing standards.

    Today

    90+ Years Strong

    Managed by the family of the late Hakeem Shaikh Mohammad Hakeem Chichi, the pharmacy serves thousands of customers daily from two Surat locations, and ships pan-India. Over 36 product categories. Still 100% natural. Still family-run.
